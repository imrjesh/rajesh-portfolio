Research positions

Postdoctoral Fellow

National Cancer Institute (NCI/NIH) · Developmental Therapeutics Branch, Center for Cancer Research · Bethesda, MD, USA · Supervisor: Dr. Anish Thomas

  • Primary computational scientist for all multi-omics analysis across SCLC clinical trials — RNA-seq, WGS, WES, ATAC-seq, ChIP-seq, scRNA-seq, spatial transcriptomics, cfDNA.
  • Novel subtype discovery: Stem-like SCLC subtype identified by integrating ATAC-seq, RNA-seq, scRNA-seq from 900+ clinical samples (Seurat, NMF, MACS2, NTP). 1st author, Cell Report Medicine 2026.

  • Managed dbGAP/SRA submissions for 5+ large-scale clinical genomics projects.
  • Optimized GATK4/Mutect2 somatic variant-calling pipelines for cfDNA and WES across multiple immunotherapy trials.
  • NIH FARE Award 2025 — Fellows Award for Research Excellence.
